Purpose of Government Websites

Government websites in India are designed to offer transparency, ease of access, and digital services to citizens, businesses, and administrative departments. These websites are part of India's ambitious Digital India initiative, which aims to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. Through these digital platforms, the government provides essential services, information dissemination, grievance redressal mechanisms, and administrative tools that streamline governance and improve citizen engagement.

Major Categories of Indian Government Websites

Informational Portals

These portals provide valuable information, including news, government schemes, and important updates.

  • india.gov.in - The National Portal of India
  • mygov.in - Citizen Engagement Platform
  • pressinformationbureau.gov.in - Government Press Releases

Service Portals

These portals allow citizens to access various online government services.

  • aadhaar.uidai.gov.in - Aadhaar Services
  • incometaxindiaefiling.gov.in - Income Tax Filing
  • passportindia.gov.in - Passport Services

Administrative Portals

Designed for government staff to carry out inter-departmental processes and manage data.

  • eoffice.gov.in - Paperless office solution
  • pgportal.gov.in - Grievance Redressal Portal

Recruitment & Education

These portals provide job opportunities, exam results, and application forms.

  • upsc.gov.in - Union Public Service Commission
  • ssc.nic.in - Staff Selection Commission
  • nta.ac.in - National Testing Agency

Grievance Redressal

Platforms where citizens can file complaints regarding government services.

  • pgportal.gov.in - Public Grievance Portal
  • e-daakhil.nic.in - Consumer Redressal Portal
